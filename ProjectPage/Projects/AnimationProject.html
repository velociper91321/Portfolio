Animation to Showcase Cascadeur to Unreal Engine workflow

Creating scene-ready animations easily

Physics-based Animation workflow
As a programmer in Unreal Engine, there will be times where we'll require specific animations. The Unreal Marketplace might not be able to give what we require, specifically. That, or finding one that's extremely expensive. And thus, in search of an answer, I stumbled upon Cascadeur.
Lo, and behold. It's physics-based animations are what saved me in my times of crisis. Easy to use with it's AI-assisted tools; even I, who's never done animation before, managed to create a decent piece of work.




Having a good reference point for your animation is crucial
following natural movements in animation ensures that the animation doesn't fall into the uncanny-valley pitfall. A reference makes sure that the animation is grounded in realistic motion and gives consistency when having a good reference for the motions. It makes the animation believeable.
